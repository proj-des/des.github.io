Tteokkbokki

Ingredients:

  • Rice cakes
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 1/2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 tsp minced garlic

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the rice cakes in boiling water for 1.5 minutes.
  2. Drain water & run cold water on the rice cakes. Pat dry.
