Peach Galette Pie

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 1 1/3 all-purppose flour
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 8 tbsp unsalted butter, cold + 1 tbsp cubed
  • 7 tbsp of cold water (or until desired consistency)
  • 1 egg, beaten (to brush the crust)
  • coarse sugar (for the crust)

For the peach filling:

  • 1 lb of peaches, 3-4 medium-sized peaches
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter, cold, pieces

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add cubes of butter. Mix so that butter is peas-sized.
  3. Then, add 1 tbsp of water between mixes. Dough is ready when it clumps together
  4. Form dough into a disk. Dust with flour and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  5. Make the peach filling. Wash and cut your peaches into slices and place in a bowl.
  6. In a different bowl, mix sugar, cinnamon, and flour. Then, sprinkle all of the mixture on the peaches.
  7. Then, mix so the flour and sugar is about evenly coated on the peaches. Then, add the vanilla extract and mix again.
  8. Preset the oven to 425 F.
  9. Get out the dough and roll it into a circle so that the diameter is 12 inches.
  10. Place the peach on the dough, leaving a 2-inch gap from the edges.
  11. Get the edges and place it in top of the peaches, almost like a pizza. Patch any holes or cracks in the crust.
  12. Place 1/2 inch butter cubes on the peaches.
  13. Place the galette into the oven for 25-30 minutes.
  14. Enjoy!!!
